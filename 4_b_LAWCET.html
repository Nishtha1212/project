TS LAWCET EXAM
  • The Telangana State Law Common Entrance Test (TS LAWCET)
  • Andhra Pradesh also conducted LAWCET exam named as APLAWCET.
  • It is a state-level exam conducted online for admission to 3-year or 5-year LLB courses in Law colleges in Telangana.
  • The Convener, Osmania University, Hyderabad conducts TS LAWCET 2024 on behalf of the Telangana State Council of Higher Education (TSCHE).
  • The qualifying marks for the examination are 35% marks for the General and OBC categories.
