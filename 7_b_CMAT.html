CMAT EXAM
  • Common Management Admission Test (CMAT) is an online computer-based test conducted by the National Testing Agency (NTA), India.
  • It is a national-level admission test for facilitating institutions.
  • To select suitable students for admission in all management programmes approved by AICTE.
  • The first edition of CMAT was conducted in 2012. In 2014, the first CMAT was conducted between 20 February and 24 February.
