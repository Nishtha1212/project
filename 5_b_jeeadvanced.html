JEE Advanced EXAM
  • Joint Entrance Examination - Advanced (JEE-Advanced) (formerly the Indian Institute of Technology - Joint Entrance Examination (IIT-JEE))
  • It is an academic examination held annually in India.
  • It is organised by one of the seven zonal IITs (IIT Roorkee, IIT Kharagpur, IIT Delhi, IIT Kanpur, IIT Bombay, IIT Madras, and IIT Guwahati) under the guidance of the Joint Admission Board (JAB)
  • It used to be the sole prerequisite for admission to the Indian Institutes of Technology bachelor's programs before the introduction of UCEED
  • JEE Advanced Exam Highlights:
  • Particulars Details
  • Full Exam Name >> Joint Entrance Examination - Advanced
  • Short Exam Name >> Jee Adavnced
  • Managing By >> Indian Institute of Technology Kanpur, Madras, Guwahati
  • Exam Level >> National
  • Languages >> English and Hindi
  • Mode of Application >> Online
  • Application Fee (Male/Female) >> 2800/1400
  • Mode of Exam >> Online
  • Mode of Question Papers >> MSQ/MCQ/NAT
  • Offered >> Once in a year
  • Exam Duration >> 2 papers of 3 hours each
