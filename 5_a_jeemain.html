JEE Main EXAM
  • Joint Entrance Examination - Main (JEE-Main), formerly All India Engineering Entrance Examination (AIEEE)
  • It is an Indian standardised computer-based test for admission to variouTs technical undergraduate programs in engineering
  • The exam is conducted by the National Testing Agency for admission to B.Tech, B.Arch, etc. in IITs and NITs.
  • It is usually conducted twice every year.
