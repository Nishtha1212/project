AIIMS EXAM
  • AIIMS stands for All India Institute Of Medical Science and it is headquartered in New Delhi, India.
  • This establishment was the initiative under the act of parliament and was soon recognized as an institution of national importance
  • It conducts entrance examination every year for the admission of students in AIIMS MBBS courses.
  • AIIMS syllabus comprises topics covered in class 11 and 12 Physics, chemistry, and biology along with General Knowledge.
