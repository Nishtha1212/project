BITSAT EXAM
  • BITSAT held as a computer-based test for all eligible candidates tentatively in two sessions.
  • To participate in the examination and admission process, the candidates will have to fill out and submit the BITSAT 2024 application form before the final date.
  • The moderated scores obtained in the entrance exam will be released through the BITSAT 2024 result/scorecard.
  • Allotment of seats will be done based on the candidate's rank, preferences and seat availability.
