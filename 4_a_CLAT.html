CLAT EXAM
  • The Common Law Admission Test (CLAT)
  • It is a centralized national-level entrance test for admissions to the 25 out of 26 National Law Universities (NLU) except NLU Delhi and NLU Meghalaya
  • CLAT was first introduced in 2008 as a centralized entrance examination for admission to the National Law Schools/Universities in India.
  • NLU Delhi takes admissions through its own test known as All India Law Entrance Test (AILET). Whereas, NLU Meghalaya was established recently and conducts its own entrance exam.
  • It is ranked one among the top five hardest entrance exam in India.
