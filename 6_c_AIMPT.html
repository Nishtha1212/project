AIPMT EXAM
  • The full form of AIPMT is the All India Pre-Medical test. CBSE oversaw this national-level admission test .
  • The admission test is now administered by the NTA and is also altered the nomenclature of the entrance exam from AIPMT to NEET (National Eligibility cum Entrance Test).
  • TThe AIPMT exam was used to select students for MBBS and BDS programs.
  • Any student may take the AIPMT (NEET) Entrance Exam three times. Every year, about 15 lakh students take the NEET-UG AIPMT (NEET-UG).
