CTET EXAM
  • The Central Teacher Eligibility Test (CTET)
  • It is an open exam for teachers conducted by the Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE).
  • It is a national-level exam that determines the eligibility of the candidates who are aspiring to be appointed as teachers
  • The examination is conducted biannually for teachers willing to teach grades 1 to 8.
  • CTET Exam Highlights:
  • Particulars Details
  • Full Exam Name >> Central Teacher Eligibility Test

  • Mode of Application >> Online

  • Mode of Exam >> Mode of Exam Paper-based Test
  • Mode of Question Papers >> MSQ/MCQ/NAT
  • Annual Number of Test Takers >> Approx. 30 lakhs
